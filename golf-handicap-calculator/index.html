Calculate Golf Handicap


Your golf handicap differential is calculated with the following formula: (18-Round Score - Course Slope) x 113 / Course Rating


The 113 represents a course rating of average difficulty, so it is a constant in the handicap formula. Once you have five round handicap differentials, your overall handicap can be calculated. It is done with the following formula: (Sum of all 5 differentials / Total Differentials) x 0.96
